Where did the votes go to?

Here are the results of the 2015 Singapore general election. 83 candidates from the People’s Action Party (PAP) and 6 candidates from the Workers’ Party (WP) were voted in to form the new parliament. The PAP was the best performing party, achieving a swing of 9.7% to take 69.9% of the popular vote.
