Who’s contesting in this year’s general election?

This visualization shows the candidates and parties contesting in this year's Singapore's 2015 General Election. Out of the 9 parties, the People's Action Party is the largest, fielding 89 candidates, followed by the Workers’ Party with 28 candidates.

The data is mainly sourced from Wikipedia and various party websites. The full list of sources can be found at the bottom of the page.
